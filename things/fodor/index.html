Jerry Fodor's Wittgenstein parody

Sometime in the 60s Michael Frayn wrote a goofy parody of Ludwig Wittgenstein’s Philosophical Investigations, and around 1970 Jerry Fodor wrote some “further addenda.” Fodor’s parody isn’t really as good, but it still deserves to be better preserved than it is at present, so I transcribed it here.
